Summer temperatures in Guntur can cross 45°C — and your heart feels the heat too.

When it's hot, your heart works harder to cool the body. This raises heart rate and blood pressure, increasing the risk of cardiac events — especially for those with existing heart conditions, hypertension, or those on blood pressure medications.

Stay safe this summer:
- Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily (coconut water and buttermilk help)
- Avoid going outdoors between 12 PM and 5 PM
- Wear loose, lightweight cotton clothing
- Monitor your blood pressure regularly
- Never skip heart medications in summer

If you experience rapid heartbeat, dizziness, chest tightness, or confusion during the heat — seek medical help immediately.
