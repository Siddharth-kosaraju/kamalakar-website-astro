April 27 – May 3 is Heart Failure Awareness Week. The theme: "Awareness Today, Protection Tomorrow."

Heart failure does NOT mean your heart has stopped. It means your heart muscle is too weak to pump blood efficiently. With early diagnosis and proper treatment, many patients live active, fulfilling lives.

Watch for these early signs:
- Breathlessness, especially when lying down
- Swollen ankles, feet, or legs
- Persistent cough or wheezing
- Unusual fatigue or weakness
- Rapid or irregular heartbeat

Common causes in India include uncontrolled high blood pressure, past heart attacks, diabetes, and valve disease.

A 2D Echo test can measure your heart's pumping strength (ejection fraction) and detect heart failure early.

If you or a loved one has these symptoms, consult a heart failure specialist.
