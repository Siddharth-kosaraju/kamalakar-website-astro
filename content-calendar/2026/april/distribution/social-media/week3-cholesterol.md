## Facebook Post
**Publish:** Tuesday, 2026-04-22

Here's a fact that surprises most people:

81.2% of Indians have some form of abnormal cholesterol — according to the ICMR-INDIAB study, one of the largest health surveys ever conducted in India.

And here's the scary part: high cholesterol has ZERO symptoms. You won't feel it. You won't see it. It silently builds up plaque in your arteries until one day — a heart attack or stroke.

Even vegetarians are at risk. The Indian diet is often high in refined carbohydrates, cooking oils, and deep-fried foods, all of which raise bad cholesterol.

What you can do:
- Replace samosas and pakoras with steamed snacks
- Use mustard oil or olive oil instead of refined oils
- Add oats, walnuts, and flaxseeds to your diet
- Get a lipid profile test — especially after age 35
